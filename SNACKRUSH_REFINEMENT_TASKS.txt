Acceptance criteria summary
- Player name can be changed by button, persists between games, and only writes finished-game scores to leaderboard.
- Leaderboard has updated 15-character base data and supports name-based completed scores cleanly.
- Main menu looks consistent, less redundant, uses better social icons/links, fixes CAS grid/control panel/leaderboard fit, and specials show tooltips.
- Skills use a single global cooldown: only one skill every 7 seconds; cooldown begins as soon as the skill starts.
- Dash replaces boost and only works while moving; dash grants 0.5 seconds invincibility frames.
- Invincibility skill lasts 3 seconds.
- Double points lasts 5 seconds and applies a lingering slowness penalty after it ends.
- Summon random special directly grants a random special effect and has a 15-second cooldown exception/override if needed.
- Combo multiplier is +1% per combo with 500% cap; combo drops after 3.5 seconds without catching candy/powerup or immediately on bad catch.
- Combo Pop area shows multiplier and displays a failed-combo visual.
- Special items have distinct design and the listed effects.
- Time Mode is 10 minutes. Freeplay uses the same mechanics but does not end by timer.
- Difficulty increases every minute, caps at minute 10, and then remains at max for Freeplay.
- Snack storms happen at 2:30, 5:00, 7:30, and 9:30, last 20 seconds, increase drops and bombs, and guarantee a powerup at storm start.
- Difficulty affects fall rate, bad item percentage, and max candy size.
- Candy size affects fall speed and value: larger = slower and more valuable; smaller = faster and lower value.
- Sounds exist with a sane mute toggle.
- SnackRush is added to GameCenter in a way that matches that repo’s architecture.
